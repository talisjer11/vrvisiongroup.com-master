Content for Education

Anatomy

An interactive anatomical atlas featuring all human body systems. This includes the human motion module, with animations of joints and bones.

Astronomy

A physics-based space simulator that allows you to create, destroy, and interact on an unimaginable scale.

Biology

A new way to experience objects by allowing you to zoom in down to the molecular level and explore the tiny alien worlds within.

Chemistry

Build organic molecules such as Carbon, Oxygen, Nitrogen and Hydrogen. Design from the bottom up using more atoms than a chemical modeling kit.

Mathematics

Intuitive ways you can learn the foundations of vector calculus. You can finally explain the complexities of vector calculus. Manipulate vectors with your hands, explore vector addition and cross product.

Geography

Explore the world from totally new perspectives in virtual reality. Stroll the streets of Tokyo, soar over the Grand Canyon, or walk around the Eiffel Tower.

Art / Design

Creating 3D paintings is every artist’s dream, and now, it is a reality. Painting Is done using a handheld “paintbrush” and the creation possibilities will be awe inspiring for any creative student.

Music

Arrange synthesizers, drums, speakers, and other equipment within the boundaries of your classroom, so you have a custom-built studio to make your own tunes.
